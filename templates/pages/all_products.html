This page displays all products currently stored in the database. The products and their associated images are retrieved through a database query and passed into Python as variables. Using Flask, Tailwind CSS, and Jinja2 templating, the products are dynamically rendered onto the page.

I haven’t implemented filters or a grid layout yet, as my focus was primarily on ensuring the functionality worked correctly.

All products are available to test using Stripe’s sandbox payment gateway. You can add items to the cart and visit the cart page to explore the payment process.
